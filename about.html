About Ceylon Sri Tours

Local Tangalle taxi & tour service with 6+ years of experience.

Ceylon Sri Tours is a small, locally owned taxi and tour service based in Tangalle, Sri Lanka. For more than six years we have been helping guests discover our beaches, villages, wildlife and culture with safe and friendly drivers.

We specialise in budget-friendly tuk tuk rides, comfortable car transfers and spacious vans for families and groups. Whether you need a quick trip around Tangalle, an airport transfer, or a full Sri Lanka round tour, we are happy to plan it together with you.

Why guests like our service

  • Experienced local drivers who speak English.
  • Clean, regularly serviced vehicles with seat belts.
  • Honest and clear pricing before every ride.
  • Flexible stops for photos, tea and local experiences.
  • Support via WhatsApp before and during your trip.
